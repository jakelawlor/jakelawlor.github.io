PNWColors

My interest in natural science is connected to my long appreciation for beauty in nature, but I find that this appreciation is often forgotten for us ecologists who conduct our research from mostly inside of doors. To remind myself of this connection and to honor my years in the most beautiful place in the world, I developed this R color palette package based off of the natural beauty of the Pacific Northwest. This was one of my first coding projects that I shared publicly, and has now been downloaded over 2700 times.

Every palette in this package is variable length and colorblind friendly for attractive and inclusive PNW-flavored data vis.

Find PNWColors on CRAN or GitHub. See link in side bar for more.
